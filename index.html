What worried parents told us they want gone

Roblox and Minecraft can be wonderful — but public servers, chat, and spending traps keep showing up in parent forums. Calm Safe City is deliberately smaller and safer.

✕Strangers & groomingNo live chat, no DMs, no public lobbies — walkers are names you type, not other players.
✕Surprise billsNo Robux, Minecoins, loot boxes, or real-money store — creative mode is free.
✕Server rouletteNo joining random worlds where rules and moderation vary block to block.
✕Bullying & griefingYour child’s city is theirs — nobody else can log in and smash it.
✕Endless “one more block”Day/night rhythm plus optional session limits and dusk save reminders.
✕Scary surprisesCartoon emergencies at most — fires can be turned off completely in calm mode.
✕Off-platform trapsNo pushes to Discord, Snapchat, or external chat apps.
✕Mod malwareRuns in the browser — nothing to download, no sketchy mod sites.
